Xbox 360 Slim

April 18th, 2011 by GuestPoster

When the Microsoft’s xbox 360 first came out just over 5 years ago it was the most powerful games console in the world and there was no equal. However scroll forward 4 years and people were getting bored of running out of space on their hard drives because of them being able to download movies and games to store there. Also there were complaints about them breaking down all the time as well as issues with the fan noise and the lack of enough ports etc so Microsoft got together and came up with the xbox 360 slim.

This new wondrous bit of kit is amazing, it comes with a 250 gb hard drive which is just over 10 times the size of the original and also comes in a nice black colour which matches all the accessories such as the controller and the black xbox 360 wireless headset. It has all the added ports that everyone wanted, you now have two extra USB ports which means a total of five should you need to run 5 things simultaneously. Touch sensitive controls add an up to date feel while it has one large fan which runs at a lower speed for increased cooling and decreased noise which is a plus for gamers everywhere. It does also run slightly faster soÂ  you will find yourself in the main menu maybe 2 seconds quicker than normal which doesn’t make a huge difference really, but is an improvement non the less.

The xbox 360 slim is just an updated version rather than a huge new machine, but with the quietness and it’s new looks along with the fact it wont overheat like the old ones it is certainly going to bring Microsoft even more success, especially with the price becomming even more competetive it is also looking like good value. So whats next for Microsoft, well there are rumours of an xbox 720 but they are just rumours and with the slim only being a few months old I wouldn’t count on it.
