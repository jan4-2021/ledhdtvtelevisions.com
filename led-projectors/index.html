LED Projectors

January 25th, 2011 by

The LED projector may be a relatively new innovation. For all that, it is one innovation that is bound to get more popular with time because of the many advantages that it obviously has. The major advantages of the LED projector include the fact that it requires little maintenance in the sense that there is no lamp to be changed. Besides, LED lights lasts much longer than other forms of light. Add the fact that there is lower usage of power and it becomes clear that the LED option is really very cost efficient. This is the precise reason LED technology is used in home theater projectors and business projectors too.

The LED projector 1080p appears to be one of the best models in the market at the moment. It combines the benefit of the latest technology with the fact the price is quite good. A very good example of this product is the HC 4000 HD projector. This product is manufactured by Mitsubishi and is highly recommended for both home theater and home entertainment use. It weighs just 7.7 lbs, has a resolution of 1920×1080 and a brightness of 1300 ANSI Lumens.

Most LED projectors use the same technology and are all great products. Therefore LED projector reviews will show that the difference between one projector and another may be based on brand name and certain specifications. There are also some differences in price as can be seen from the details below. The Samsung SP- F10M projector costs $1299 while the Epson Home Cinema 8700UB projector goes for a princely $2199. The Sony VPL-VWPRO1 Home Theater projector is also a great deal at $3400. This list is by no means exhaustive as there are quite a number of great products out there. It has to be noted though that people who want to buy projectors need to deal with authorized dealers. With great advances in science and technology, projectors are no longer as expensive as they used to be. More to the point, people are sure of getting the best LED projector in the market without having to spend a lot of money. It gets even better because the HD LED projector is also available and can be purchased at a very affordable price.

Finally, buyers need to have some basic knowledge on how these projectors work before buying them. There would be no point in buying an expensive LED projector and damaging it in the process of installation or usage. Where possible, experts need to be consulted to help the owners understand how to use these great products. Buyers will also do well to take advantage of after sales service and maintenance for best results.
