How to Find Laptops Under $500

October 2nd, 2010 by GuestPoster

There are so many great deals on laptops out there. If you are on a tight budget, but need a laptop, you may need to do some digging to get the one that you want for the price that you want to pay. Once you know where to look, though, you will be surprised just how much selection there is.

When many people want a new computer, they think it is time to head to their local Best Buy store. Best Buy has done a good job as marketing themselves as the solution to that problem. They are a great place to go to to try out different sizes and see the quality of different brands, but it would be foolish to walk out of the store with one of their laptops. You can get the same laptops online for less, even from their website. They always have special online only deals.

Amazon is another place you cannot afford to to skip. There are hundreds, if not thousands, of individual vendors that sell on Amazon. Some will be selling used or refurbished, and many sell new laptops at low prices. Since there are so many vendors selling there, they have to have competitively low prices in order to make a sale. This is good for you.

Many people don’t think of ebay, especially if they are looking for a new laptop or gaming laptops under $500. Everyone knows that ebay is the place to sell used stuff, but a lot of people are selling new stuff there, as well. There will always be ignorant bidders who drive up the price, but if your timing is good, you can get a great deal there. Sellers buy items that were overstocked, or maybe even discontinued. There are great laptops out there to be had if you look in the right places, and some will come with free laptop cases bags.
