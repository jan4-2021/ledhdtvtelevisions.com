Toshiba NB205 – N313/P Laptop

June 8th, 2010 by GuestPoster

Remarkably easy to carry, filled with smart features, rich in style and innovation, the mini NB205 lifts up the bar when it comes to portability. It has an easy to use keyboard and stellar battery time. This Toshiba trendy device from Toshiba sets itself apart from other mini laptops by good internal configuration, startling design, light weight, and extended battery life.

Design and Features:
This laptop uses Intel Atom N280 processor clocked at 1.66 GHz. Graphics are supported by 945GSE Intel graphics card, which works at data bus rate of 667 MHz. The laptop uses L2 cache memory with a capacity of 512 KB. A maximum of 2 GB of DDR2 SDRAM is supported, however 1 GB RAM is sufficient for average usage. It has a portable hard disk drive with standard storage capacity of 160 GB.

The NB205 has a 10.4 inches wide screen, with TFT display and it weighs around 2.9 lbs. It is 1.3 inches tall and 7.6 inches deep. Even with such small dimensions, it supports multimedia and SDHC memory card and Bluetooth. It also comes equipped with network adapter and supports wireless LAN. It has a sound card for high definition audio, but the quality of sound output is poor due to the non availability of good speakers in the device. It does have a decent keyboard and an integrated camera.

It uses lithium ion battery with a capacity of 5800 mAh, which makes the laptop bulky and not one of the best mini laptop 2010. But the configuration is good enough for normal home usage. The device comes with a pre-installed Windows XP Home Edition, which can be upgraded to Windows 7.

The good:
Toshiba NB205 has 9.5 hours of battery life. It has a keyboard which is very easy to use and with a large touchpad. It has excellent protection of hard drive. Even when system is switched off, the USB port can be used to charges other multimedia devices.

The bad:
This lid of the laptop attracts fingerprints and the audio output is not up to the mark. Additionally, the cost of this mini laptop is on the higher side.
