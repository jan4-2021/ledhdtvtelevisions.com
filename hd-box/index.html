May 25th, 2010 No Comments

Watching videos on a HD TV offers excellent viewing experience. The graphics are presented very clearly as the display contains up to two million pixels per frame. Nowadays, it is good to know that you can enjoy this amazing feature with your ordinary computer monitor, through the help of an HD Box. This new innovation in HD accessories supports the full 1080p HD resolution, so you can appreciate full high definition graphics even without an HD TV. This is very helpful if you want to enjoy an extraordinary viewing and gaming experience without spending a lot of money on a brand new HD TV, plus you can also use the HD set top box wherever you want.

Since a HD Box is an external accessory, you can use it in a variety situation – either through a PC monitor, an ordinary TV or a gaming console. Input mode ranges from 480i up to 1080p (60Hz/50Hz), whilst the output mode varies from 640×480 up to 1920×1200. Because of this, the HD external box can support 4:3-16:9 signals on a 5:4-16:10 widescreen monitor without compromising the picture quality so it can perfectly adjust in any kind of monitor. Along with its high resolution graphics, it can also support high quality audio outputs for a more enhanced and detailed viewing experience.

Throughout the market, the HD boxes are extensively offered with different specifications, there are brands that are equipped with special features and some come with more accessories than others. Often, a HD set top boxes come with a charger, a remote and a set of connecting cables. There also have numerous ports so give you many options on how and what you can connect it to. If planning to buy one, remember to first check if the box is compatible with your specification of monitor. Since the HD converter box has many ports, finding one that is compatible with your monitor is usually easy. So whenever you want a high definition viewing experience, it can be easily and cheaply achieved with a HD box.
