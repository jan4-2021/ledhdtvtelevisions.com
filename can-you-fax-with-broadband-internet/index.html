Can You Fax with Broadband Internet?

March 4th, 2011 by GuestPoster

Is it possible to fax with a broadband internet? Yes. With fax to email services, also known as online fax services, you can send and receive faxes via any internet connection. Yo don’t need a land line and you don’t have to install any extra phone lines.

To start faxing via the internet all you need is the just mentioned and a computer. So far very simple right? That’s all you need! Online fax services are a great faxing solution for everyone, whether you send a few faxes each month, or hundreds of faxes from your company or home office.

Internet fax services offer several plans to choose from and even pay-as-you-go providers. Since the service is based online you can access it from any internet connection via computer, laptop and even from your cell phone. Online fax services are usually compatible with both Windows and Mac. You can also cancel at anytime without having to pay a fee because there are no contracts.
