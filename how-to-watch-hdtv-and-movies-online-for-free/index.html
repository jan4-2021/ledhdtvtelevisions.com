How to Watch HDTV and Movies and Films Online for Free

May 17th, 2009 by

How do you watch HDTV online for free? It is as not complicated as you might think. First thing you need to do before you are able to watch HDTV online is to download a media player. There are many websites that offer “freeware”, which you can download software free of charge. Once you download the media player, you need to know how where to download movies, television shows and other types of media, download.com is where you download software.

Warez sites have message forums where people share their movies and you can download it for free. You have to register on the message forum before you can access the content. Many of the files on these forums are in RAR or ZIP format. You can download a program that will let you “unzip” the files. Once the files are unzipped, you can play them in your media player.

7-Zip is free software that unzips your RAR files for you. JDownloader is another free software tool to use when downloading multiple RAR files from sites such as Rapidshare and Megaupload. If you do not have time to download but want to watch HD online immediately, you can stream the movie. Watching a streaming video requires a flash player. Your internet browser should include this automatically, tv-links.cc is the most popular website where you can download movies and watch HD TV online.

Other ways to watch HDTV online is to download movies from bittorrent sites and P2P (peer-to-peer) sites. You need a bittorrent client such as Azureus Vuze to download a torrent. P2P sites such as Frostwire and Ares Destiny have all types of movies you can download for free. When you download movies from bittorrent sites and P2P sites, make sure you have anti-virus software. There are stupid people online that want to do harmful things to your computer. You have to be cautious. If you choose to download a bittorrent, browse the comment section. The comment section will let you know if the file you are downloading is malicious and/or fake.
