LG Infinia LV5500 Smart LED TV Review

January 13, 2012

If you’re looking for a top, big brand LED Smart TV at an affordable price, then the LG Infinia LV5500 fits the bill. It offers big screen entertainment, with a full compliment of technical specifications including a 120Hz super thin display, Wi-Fi and Smart TV.Not bad considering it’s one of the cheapest big name TVs in this class.

Read the rest of this LG Infinia LV5500 review to find out more.

LG Infinia 42LV5500 – 42 Inch LED TV
LG Infinia 47LV5500 – 47 Inch LED TV

The LG Infinia LV5500 is available in three different display sizes. This LG LV5500 review is applicable for all the models in the series as it is only the display size which differs, other than this all of the technical specifications are identical.

LG LV5500 Video Performance

The LG LV5500 series video performance on the whole is very good considering its small price. As a whole the color reproduction and gamma levels are very good once configured optimally. This is something I have found to be one of LGs strong points, you are able to achieve a great setup using LG”s advanced settings. Also LG’s out of the box Cinema mode produces goods results.

LG LV5500 Design and Styling

What can I say about the LV5500âs design? Its black and rectangular and I like it! The LED panel depth measures 1 inch in depth which is thin, and so it would look great when wall mounted. Also the swivel stand which it sits on feels reassuringly sturdy when you need to rotate the display.



LG LV5500 Usability

The LV5500 has many different cool features and settings which can be adjusted through the menu system, something which is typically a strong point with LG TVs. It results in a better picture setup than the in-built standard preset modes do, so in my opinion this is a great addition.

As well as this feature the LG LV5500 has a 10-point calibration system for more advanced users to twiddle with. Other features include de-judder for those viewers who like their movies to appear smooth (no to everyone’s taste) and a 24p Real Cinema Mode.

I love this method of operation combine with LG’s menus because it makes the TV breeze to control.

On a final note, the LED display has a matte finish to the surface which means there is little or no glare from surrounding light sources.

LG LV5500 Connectivity

The LV5500 includes a generous selection of connectivity options and doesn’t leave anything out. Importantly it has 4 HDMI inputs, 2 USB ports with a Wi-Fi dongle included as well an Ethernet connection for Internet connectivity. As well as this the LV5500 has a PC video connection, 2 composite inputs and 1 component input.

LG LV5500 Smart TV Features

As already mentioned above this TV comes with both wireless and Ethernet connections meaning it can be hooked up to the Internet however you please. Combine this with the Magic Motion remote control, the slick menu system and a complete range of Internet services and you definitely have one of the best Smart TV models in this price bracket.

LG Smart TV provides you with all of the major Internet Apps and services such as Netflix, Hulu, Amazon Instant, Vudu, Facebook and an integrated web browser.

If your interested, you can also hook up your other media (video, music and pictures) from laptops, cameras and removable memory by either using DLNA in conjunction with the network connectivity or by connecting storage devices to one of the USB ports.
