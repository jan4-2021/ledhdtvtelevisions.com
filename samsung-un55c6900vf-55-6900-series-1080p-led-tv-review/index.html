Samsung UN55C6900VF 55″ 6900 Series 1080p LED TV Review

March 12th, 2011 by admin

The Samsung Samsung UN55C6900 55 inch 6900 Series LED HDTV has a lot to offer. It has numerous features and gets top ratings for being a well-made, easy to use, high quality LED TV. As this Samsung UN55C6900VF 55″ Class 6900 Series 1080p LED HDTV review demonstrates, there are many advantages to purchasing this particular model. The Samsung 6900 Series LED TV has superb sound and high quality, realistic picture. It is sleek, stylish and lightweight. It comes with a swivel, installation instructions and how to videos.

The Samsung 6900 Series 1080p LED TV also comes with numerous features. Those who enjoy gaming are sure to appreciate the fact that this particular LED TV can easily be connected to the internet and that it has an easy to use built in game mode. Any gamer will be enthralled by the clarity and surround sound that Samsung 6900TV provides.

Some of the Samsung 55″ LED HDTV’s many other features include excellent aspect ratio, high refresh rate, an optional linkstick adapter, wide color enhancement, picture in picture, timer, closed captioning, PC audio input, two USB ports, headphone jack, audio out and Ethernet. Despite the fact that the Samsung 55″ LED TV has so much to offer, it is not expensive. In fact, it is quite affordable considering the many technological features that come with it. This Samsung LED TV is a durable model and is not only Energy Star compliant but far exceeds standards.

Anyone who enjoys watching TV will find the Samsung 55″ LED HDTV to be a good investment of money. It takes a bit of initial time and work to set the TV up properly, but it is time well spent. Samsung has a good track record for creating high quality appliances and equipment, but it seems that the company has outdone itself with this particular LED TV. The picture quality goes beyond good; it is simply out of this world. Many users have commented that when watching something on this TV, it feels ultra realistic. The build in sound on this TV is excellent, although some have also purchased the optional Samsung sound bar for additional, louder surround sound.

As one can see from reading this Samsung UN55C6900VF 55 inch 6900 Series LED TV Review, it is hard to go wrong buying this TV. The Samsung 55″ 6900 Series TV is one of the best options on the market. It is sturdy and durable, yet sleek and classy. It comes with a swivel stand and a large quantity of features. It does not matter if a person wants to watch a TV show, sports, a DVD, bluray, hook up to the internet or play a game. This TV enables one to do it all. Samsung has truly made a winner here, as thousands of people the world over can attest to. The TV features are easy to program and work with. Overall, this TV is exceedingly fantastic value for the pricetag.
