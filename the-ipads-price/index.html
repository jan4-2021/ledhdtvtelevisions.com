The iPad’s Price

March 4th, 2010 by GuestPoster

The iPad price is unbelievable. The price range is from $499 to $829. The prices for the iPad that has 16GB of memory and Wi-Fi is $499. The iPad that has 32GB of memory and has Wi-Fi is $599. The iPad that has 64GB of memory and Wi-Fi is $699. The other thing that you can have for your iPad besides Wi-Fi is having a 3G network. The downside to the 3G network is the fact that the 3G network doesn’t let you make calls, it only lets you connect to cell phone towers to retrieve and send infomation over the net. But other than that, the following prices are the other prices. The iPad that has 16GB of memory, has Wi-Fi, and has a connection to the 3G network is $629. The iPad that has 32GB of memory, has Wi-Fi, and has a connection to the 3G network is $729. The most expensive iPad that Apple currently is going on the market is priced at $829, it has 64 GB of memory, has Wi-Fi, and has a connection to the 3G network. They are all a good deal and are good choices to choose from while the iPad it self is just a great device. This outstanding device is so good that even if you decide to buy the 32GB device you will still have a great experience with it.

There are just a few downsides about this that might just answer your biggest question of all, “When is it going to be available to buy?” and “When will the international prices be released?” The answer to both of those questions would be later. It is planning to be sold at the end of March. The Apple rumor mill unfortunately doesn’t have a better time frame then that at this point. The 3G version will come out later then that, sometime in April perhaps.
