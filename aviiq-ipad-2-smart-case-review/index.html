Aviiq Ipad 2 Smart Case Review

April 5th, 2011 by GuestPoster

Do you own an Ipad 2? Have you been browsing the internet for Ipad cases and covers trying to look for one that is affordable yet stylish and durable? If you’ve just answered yes, you should consider Aviiq’s Smart Case for Ipad 2, trust me, you will not regret it, it’s one of the best Ipad accessories that you’ll ever find nowadays.

Just like you, I was also trying to look for a case online even before the Ipad 2 was released (yes, I was that excited). I visited the multiple websites until I ended up on their Aviiq’s website. I was pretty impressed with the design so I bookmarked the page and ordered it online after I was able to buy an Ipad 2.

When I received the Aviiq smart case, I Immediately noticed that it’s very light and I have found out later that it only weighs 4.1 ounces. It’s sides are made of a gray plastic material which I think is good enough to protect your Ipad 2. Its back is made of thin aluminum so if you’re worried about getting scratches at the back of your Ipad 2, you should buy one of these. One thing I really liked about this Ipad case is that it’s not hard to install unlike the other models that are available these days.

Ipad owners will also appreciate its size, it’s not bulky so it can easily be inserted in a bag. I’m pretty impressed with the size of its aluminum casing which is only 1.5 mm thick, this means that I’ll be able to hold it firmly.

Where to buy the Aviiq smart case for Ipad 2?

You can buy this from their website (aviiq[dot]com) for only $49.99. Some may find it expensive especially when they know that they can buy other Ipad cases for less than $20 bucks. However, if you’re shopping for a case, you need to consider the quality and the design as well, not just the price.
