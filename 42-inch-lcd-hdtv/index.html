Toshiba 42 Inch LCD HDTV

March 22nd, 2010 by

There are a range of 42 inch LCD HDTVs on sale at many online stores, and the price keeps going down regularly. Toshiba is a leader in producing LCD HDTVs which have a 1080p full high definition display and can have features such as clear scan 240 pure pixel, auto view, cine speed LCD panel, backlight control, native mode, cinema mode, color temperature control, crystal coat high contrast and so much more.

Toshiba takes its Series of LCD HDTV’s to the next level with technological advances in both audio and video to make the most of your home theatre experience. If you are a fan of sports, video games, movies you will be stunned by the clear scan 240 advanced frame rate technology, which has been greatly improved from previous versions of LCD HDTV. The latest version includes Dolby volume technology for a consistent audio level when changing the channel or when a program switches to commercials.

It includes many connection options so your TV can be used as a PC monitor or gaming TV, other features include a dynamic backlight control for deeper black levels and four digital inputs for simple connections to cable or satellite boxes, DVD players and more. And they also meet energy star’s certifications for reducing energy consumption by 25%.

With full high definition, there is no need to scale down a 1080 signal. With twice the pixel resolution of 720pixel high definition models, full HD is an absolute master when it comes to picture quality. Clear scan’s screen technology massively improves on older LCD panels by combining the LED backlighting to create significantly clearer full motion video.
