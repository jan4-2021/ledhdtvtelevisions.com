Mounting Your T.V. at Home

March 9th, 2010 No Comments

Mounting your television onto the wall yourself, instead of hiring a professional, can be a great way to save money. However, it is only a great way to save money if you have done a little research, and have the right tools and know how to do a good job. If you consider yourself a handy person, and have a good tool box, you should be able to finish this project without much difficulty.

First things first, it is important that you have carefully inspected the wall that you want to mount your television on, to make sure that is has the ability to support the weight of your new t.v. It is very important to make sure that you find the studs in your wall, and do not try to randomly hang your television onto any old spot that you please. That would be very dangerous, and would most definitely result in huge holes in your wall. Another thing to consider, it the aesthetics of the room, and how your placement can add to that. It is important to measure accurately, and make sure that you have a tool that can help you determine that your television mount is level. You will want to make sure that you have all of the supplies necessary to provide a stable place for your television to hang. This will include a mounting plate that will be attached to your television, and a mounting base that it will be attached to on your wall. You will also need screws, a screw driver, and a drill. It is a good idea to attach screws after you have mounted the television onto the wall, as well, just as an added security.

There are many mounting kits on the market, and you can order them online, or pick up your supplies at a home improvement store. .Â  Having a partner to help you, is also a good way to ensure that the job gets done quickly, and can help prevent injuries, as well.
