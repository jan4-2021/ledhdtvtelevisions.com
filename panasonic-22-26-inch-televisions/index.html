Panasonic 22 and 26 Inch Televisions

May 27th, 2010 by GuestPoster

Probably the best best 22 in LCD TV in town today, theVIERA TC L22X2 22″ from Panasonic provides excellent picture quality that can not be matched by other manufacturers. It has 720p High Definition quality picture. A great additional feature of this model is a universal dock for an Apple iPod which will allow you to play music and video directly from your iPod or your iPhone. It also has a playback function when playing movies from a DVD or Blu Ray disc player. For gamers, it also boasts of a special Game Mode that allows excellent response when playing 3D games from consoles. The VIERA TC L22X2 22″ also has a high resolution when it comes to moving pictures that allows it to display bright and ultra clear images. With this model Panasonic has demonstrated why they are one of the leading TV manufacturers in the world today.

It does not end there though. Panasonic has another winner in the category of 26″ televisions. Their 26 ” TC- LCD HDTV is also another great performer when compared with other similar models from other manufacturers. The LX70 has an advanced panel that allows the viewers to see the images perfectly regardless of the angle of where you are seated from the screen. The revolutionary technology bypasses one of the severe limitations of flat screen TVs and combines with the best feature of CRT television. With the 26″ LX70 you don’t have to worry about where you are seated, because you can enjoy the show wherever you are. An additional feature with this model is the advanced color technology used in it that allows you to view shows with realistic color. It’s like your seeing the shows in real life.

Those are just two of the televisions from Panasonic, and they have a lot more. Go and check them out!
