Westinghouse LED TV Review Summary

When you read the Westinghouse LED TV reviews posted by owners online there are a few things to note. The first is that the majority of the consumers are happy with the picture quality produced. Many are un-happy with the sound quality produced. Another minor criticism is that the remote controls are not very responsive and have a poor range.

Many consumers don’t realise that even many of the leading brands models of slim LED TVs also have poor sound quality. This is due to the lack of available space to include a decent set of speakers.Having said that, the sound produced should at least be acceptable without having to install an additional surround system. Most leading brands of televisions do actually fit in to the ‘acceptable’ category. However, Westinghouse LED TVs sound quality is quite bad, and you will certainly benefit from hooking it up to a hifi or surround system if possible.

When Westinghouse TVs are half the price of the major brands equivalent models, it is hard to be over critical. Even though they are never going to out-perform the more expensive, leading TV brands they have get a thumbs-up based on value for money alone, they offer a way to achieve a big-screen home cinema setup for consumers with a small budget.
