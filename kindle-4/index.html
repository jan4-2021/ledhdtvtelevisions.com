The New Kindle 4

January 14th, 2011 by GuestPoster

There is no doubt that a lot of people are waiting very eagerly for the new Kindle 4 to be released. We will find that without doubt it is going to be a great new Amazon item that will see a massive number of sales. Online there is a lot of speculation about just what it is that we are actually going to finally get. Will it be touchscreen, color, or something else entirely?

The truth is that no one really knows just what it will really be like. It may well have a touch screen but it seems a lot more likely that it will be a color Kindle 4. The technology, thanks to the E Ink company, is already in place for e-book readers to be made with a color display. It may not quite be up to laptop or tablet standards but it does work.

Although the quality is slightly lower the main advantage is that this technology allows the Kindle to still have a battery life that is measured in weeks rather than hours. And we can guarantee that this will be its unique selling point. No doubt a vast number will be sold and it does remain to be seen just how good the final product will be.

If you are waiting to buy the new Kindle then the unfortunate truth is that you will have to wait for some time yet. It will probably not be until the very end of 2011 that it becomes available so until then it is a matter of waiting or buying the excellent Kindle 3 instead.

The Kindle 3 is hugely popular and it really is a joy to be able to get a book on to the Kindle in less than a minute. It is very exciting to see just what is going to be released for the 4th version.
