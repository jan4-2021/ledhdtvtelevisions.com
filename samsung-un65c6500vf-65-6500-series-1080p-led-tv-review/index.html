Samsung UN65C6500VF 65″ 6500 Series 1080p LED TV Review

March 12th, 2011 by admin

The Samsung UN65C6500VF 65″ LED TV is one of the newest, most advanced and largest models on the market. It is more expensive than the average LED HDTV but has so much to offer that the price is, comparatively speaking more than quite reasonable. As this Samsung UN65C6500VF 65″ Class 6500 Series 1080p LED HDTV review will bring out, the Samsung 6500 Series LED TV is a good buy for those who are looking for a large screen, high quality TV with numerous options.

The Samsung 65″ LED TV comes with hundreds of web apps. One can watch a movie either from Bluray, DVD or the internet with ease. This TV is perfect and can be easily used for gaming purposes. The TV can be connected to more than one computer at a time and will display photos from a camera or mobile phone as well. Other features of the Samsung 65″ LED HDTV include an apps platform, DLNA network capabilities, BD wise, Any Net Plus, auto power off, volume levelling, timer, built in tuning, V chip, four HDMI inputs, computer audio input and video input, an Ethernet port, USB ports and RF input.

The Samsung 6500 has very high quality picture and sound. The color is bright and realistic and can easily be adjusted or tweaked as needed. The Samsung 6500 Series LED TV is not 3D but is uses a state of the art 3D color stretching technology that saturates each pixel and enhances the quality of picture. The built in bottom speakers use Surround Sound to provide clear and powerful sound.

While most large screen LED HDTV’s consume a lot of power, this is not the case with the Samsung 6500 Series TV. In fact, this TV exceeds Energy Star’s recommendations by over 40% and is therefore more energy efficient than other large screen TV models. While most people do not consider the environmental impact of purchasing a large screen TV, it is nice to see that Samsung takes its work seriously and provides a TV that is not only top of the line but also environmentally friendly as well. Do not simply write this fact off as a marketing ploy, for a display of this size over its lifetime it will save you considerable amounts of money on your electricity bill and more than pay for the investment. The attention to detail is a sign that the company produces top quality products.

As this Samsung UN65C6500VF 65 inch 6500 Series LED HDTV review illustrates, one does get an awful lot for the purchase price. While the Samsung 6500 Series LED TV is an expensive model (it is after all a top of the range LED TV that is as big as they come), it is tremendous value for the money. The TV is sleek, lightweight and stylish. It is sure to compliment the decor of any living room. More importantly, the Samsung UN65C6500 has extremely high quality sound and pictures of which you can not appreciate unless you see it with your own eyes. Samsung has used state of the art technology to create a TV that is a pure pleasure to watch, listen to music on, view photos or use for gaming. This LED TV is simply one of the very best options on the market.
