Review Rage 2011: A Nintendo 3DS Review

January 24th, 2011 by GuestPoster

Games nowadays are so vast and multidimensional that it is growing more difficult to peg down games to a single genre. However, games are sometimes cycled over and over again to milk it for all its worth before killing it and creating a new one in almost the same format, with almost the same gameplay and almost the same story line, with the exception of the title and probably the names of all the characters and the places and things involved. A few good examples of these kinds of games are the ones that copied the immensely popular Grand Theft Auto franchise. Because of this revolutionary sand box genre, several game developers have come up with their own versions such as Mafia, Red Faction, and Saint’s Row.

So why not suggest to Nintendo to create a Nintendo 3DS review game, a pioneering game that is based on the classic formula of Nintendo 3DS games: Easy on the eyes, intuitive, frustratingly challenging and very addicting.
