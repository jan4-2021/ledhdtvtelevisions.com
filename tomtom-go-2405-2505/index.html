Qualities of the TomTom Go 2405 and 2505

by GuestPoster in Other Technology


TomTom is one of the topmost providers of high quality devices that are related to the field of navigation and communication. Millions of people all over the world make use of the products manufactured by this company, an approximated 45 million. These two gadgets have compact sizes that make them extremely easy to bring around and store. Both of them possess a glass, touch capable display however the sizes differ. The Go 2405 has a screen which measures 4 inches. On the other hand, the 2505 has one that measures 5 inches. Another similarity is that the screens of both devices are glossy and this sometimes causes a few individuals to have concerns with glare.

Protecting the Go 2405 and 2505 and making it more durable is the metal frame. A car cradle design is also something that these gadgets can offer. It makes handling very easy and allows for easy removal and detachment of the device. These TomTom portable GPS systems are also of compact size making it easy for them to fit inside a purse or in your pocket. Added to this is the locked on magnetic power cord. The speakers which can be found at the back panel produce nothing but the clearest sounds. Also acting as a charging indicator is the power button. This is significant because it informs the owner when the device has been charged fully.

Connecting the TomTom Go 2405 and 2505 to a computer can be done by way of a USB adapter since it lacks an SD and a micro SD card slot. These gadgets are fitted with a modified and improved software which enables the owner to completely get the most out of the touch attributes of the glass displays. This is significantly faster than the previously used software. The OS which is WebKit based also makes navigating through the various functions and features of these portable GPS systems very easy. The user will also be consistently informed of any relevant traffic information and this includes the provision of map updates. Map screens are also available for the users.
