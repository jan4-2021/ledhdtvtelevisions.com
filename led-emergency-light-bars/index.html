LED Emergency Light Bars

December 26th, 2010 by GuestPoster

Are you looking to find out more about LED Emergency light bars for your emergency vehicle? If so then this is going to be the article for you. Emergency light bars are typically used to provide lights typically for emergency medical services, police officers, construction crews, and even some government cars.

There are different types of LED emergency light bars available. A not so typical type is the amber light bar. This is the kind of light bar you find on towing vehicles as well as your construction crews doing road repairs. It comes with two types of kits to install it. Mounting kit, which you use screws to attach it to a bar on top of your vehicle. The other is magnetic strip kit. This means it will not ruin the top of your vehicle just in case you use it for more than one thing.

Another type of emergency light bars you may find is the typical police bar. These bars are mainly used for police cars as well as some emergency medical services vehicles. These bars are usually placed atop the hood with either mounting system and are used for an array of emergencies. These lights are typically not allowed to be used by any other type of vehicle, other than emergency vehicles. That way it can be distinguished from other cars and to stop criminals from pretending to be a police officer.

Another type of popular LED emergency light bars is the color combination of amber and clear. These lights provide some emergencies from being seen from miles away so as not to cause any other types of accidents. These as well come with both mounting styles and provide complete instructions on how to install them.

These types of lights are usually frowned upon unless you are an emergency vehicle and have a valid reason for having them installed on your vehicle. Checking your states laws will help to ensure you are within the laws to have them upon your car or truck. Doing that, will keep you in line and have your vehicle rolling along in style.
