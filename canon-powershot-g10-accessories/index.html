Canon PowerShot G10 Accessories

August 15th, 2010 by GuestPoster

Buying a digital camera involves a lot of searching on the internet. People used to spend couple of weeks reading the reviews and recommendations from experts before buying a digital camera. Once you have brought a camera, again you have to spend time to look for the accessories that you want for this camera. Since accessories are made specific to camera models, you should check the compatibility before buying the particular accessory. One way to avoid this additional search is buy buying camera and accessories together in one package, generally termed as digital camera bundles.

In this article, we will talk about some of the basic and high end accessories compatible with Canon PowerShot G10 – one of the best selling point and shoot camera with features of a digital SLR camera. Some of the accessories for Canon PowerShot G10 digital camera are:-

1) Leather Camera Case – To protect your camera from scratches and external elements, this camera case is very useful. Model number for this item is SC – DC60 and will cost around 50$ or less.

2) Additional car adaptor to recharge your batteries on the move, so that you will never miss a chance to click pictures.

3) Additional camera battery

4) Waterproof camera case to use the camera under water (make the camera waterproof). By using this camera case you can click photos underwater up to the depth of 40 meters. This case costs around 150$ and the item code is WP-DC28.

5) Remote shutter release (RS60 E3) to release the camera shutter remotely and will cost not more than 25$.

6) PowerShot G10 ring kit – This accessory comes with three different color rings. So that you can replace the original black color with these add-ons and without costing the earth.

7) Speedlite Transmitter (ST E2) can be used to wirelessly control the two different set of external flashes. Speedlite transmitter for Canon PowerShot G10 ranges from 120$ to 350$.

And many more accessories are available depending on the specific need.
