Cases and Screen Protectors For Your iPad

September 7th, 2010 by GuestPoster

Apple’s iPad is a wonderful device a feat of engineering and design with a great interface. But given its portability and the “casual” nature of the device, it’s likely that you won’t be overprotective of it. Over a number of years of use, your iPad may become scratched, dented and scuffed. Even if you’re careful with it, your friends, family and colleagues may not be. Not everyone is as kind to electronic devices as you.

For this reason, you might look into iPad cases. An iPad case is a great choice if you want to extend the longevity of your tablet. It can help to keep it clean and undamaged, both from minor scuffs and even major impacts – a case with some give in it will offer some protection if it happens to suffer a fall. There is a wide range of iPad cases available and here are just a few, to give you an idea of the variety.

Quirky Cloak This is a simple design made from non-slip rubber. It’s neat in that it features a stand that allows the iPad to stand up on its own in either portrait or landscape mode. The hinges are of durable plastic and the screen can be positioned at various angles.

Vers Wood Case This is a very elaborate case, ideal for someone who wants to make a statement, especially if you care about the environment. The Vers Wood Case is handcrafted from bamboo and hardwoods and also offers a stand.

BlytheKing iSocket Etsy These cases are really cool they’re made from repurposed vintage fabrics! A great soft case.

You might also look into iPad screen protectors. These can not only protect the screen from nicks and scratches (bound to accumulate through regular use) but can also function as an anti-glare film, to counter Apple’s insistence on high-gloss screen finishes.
