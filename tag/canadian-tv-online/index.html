Watching Canadian TV Online

No comments

TV is one of the most successful and effective ways of transmitting relevant and significant news. Now, you can watch TV even when you are online. If you live in Canada, there are lots of different ways you can access Canadian TV online.

Channels like CBC channels, Toronto TV, and many more are available online, as long as your computer system supports the application. Commonly they need a Windows or Linux application. These online TV channels feature the latest news on everything that is newsworthy in Canada.

There are several TV channels in Canada that you could watch online free:

Canal Films Francophones
It features almost 5,000 channels and related channels are APN movies, AFN movies, and short films. There are also comedy movies, Pino di Maio of Italy and many others. You could also watch live sports like football online.

CBC Toronto
This channel broadcasts from Toronto featuring the latest news, weather forecasts, the juiciest tidbit in entertainment and more. It has related are CBC Calgary, WebMirTV in Russia, CBC Ottawa News and several others.

CBC Montreal
Updates you on the latest development in the city of Montreal. The daily regular programming features movies, sports, news and other news worthy events. Related channels are CBC, The National, Toronto TV ch3, CityNews to name a few.

City News
Features pre-recorded online news on movies, entertainment, sports, lifestyles, recent happenings and the like. Related channels are CBC Ottawa News, CBC NewFoundland and others. They record developments on headlines stories and keep you posted.

CBC Windsor
Delivering the daily news at Windsor, this channel updates residents of news worthy items in the locality. Related channels are CBC Toronto, CPAC French and many others.

AfriqueCanada TV
This channel makes use of pre-recorded videos to present updates on movies, culture and sports in Canada, Africa and in other parts of the world. Related channels are CBC National, Argent, Shopping TVA and others.

The major channels that provide Canadian TV online services are CBC and CTV. Both have a wide spectrum of shows starting from comedy shows, to sports, to entertainment, culture, discovery channel, comedy and a lot more.
