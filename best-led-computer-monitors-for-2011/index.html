Best LED Computer Monitors For 2011

July 4th, 2011 by admin

Finding the best LED computer monitor is almost an impossible task. With so many brands and models of computer displays saturating the marketplace where do you begin? When buying an LED computer monitor the easiest way to start is by narrowing down your options, set yourself a budget for how much you are prepared to spend and if possible what size LED computer display you require.

Buying an LED computer monitor is wise choice compared to buying a CCFL LCD monitor because the benefits of a computer display that uses LED lighting are similar to the benefits attributed to LED TVs. Typically LED monitors have slimmer designs, have longer lifespans, are more reliable, better energy efficiency ratings and they do not use mercury in their production and so are more environment friendly.

Another benefit of LED computer monitors is that they also tend to be slightly easier to view, especially for long periods of time. When using a computer monitor you tend to look at it more intensely and for longer periods than you would a TV and this can cause eye strain and headaches. So having a display which reduces these problems can be a big advantage.

Buying the best LED computer monitor might mean you spending a little bit more than if you were to buy an equivalent standard LCD monitor (that uses fluorescent lighting), but as the cheapest LED computer monitors start at around $100 you will definitely be able to find a model to match your budget no matter how large or small that might be.
