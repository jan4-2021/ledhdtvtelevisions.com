Budget Plasma TV Models 2011

March 21st, 2011 by GuestPoster

Everyone knows that a 42 inch plasma TV will provide a great audio visual experience. The only problem people find about this device is that it can cost quite a lot. This might hold true a couple of years ago however with the advances made in technology; there are models that are priced at an affordable rate. If you are planning to buy the best plasma TV 2011 then try to consider this updated list of 42 inch budget models.

Zenith P42W46X 42-Inch Flat Panel Plasma
Zenith is one of the most trusted brands when it comes to TV models and their Zenith P42W46X plasma TV is one of the cheapest models in the market that comes with high quality. This model is capable of projecting videos in 16:9 widescreen modes at 852x480p resolutions. You get vivid colors when watching videos owing to its 5,000:1 contrast ratio. You can even connect your PC to this TV to enjoy playing your favorite games on the large screen.

Samsung PN42C430 42-Inch 720p Plasma HDTV
When you are looking for a 42 inch plasma TV that can provide a much higher resolution then you should try to consider the Samsung PN42C430. This model is capable of displaying 720p videos easily which provide a much more detailed picture to the viewer. Panasonic TC-P42C2 42-Inch 720p Plasma HDTV
Last on our list for budget 42 inch plasma TV is the Panasonic TC-P42C2. This slim model makes it excellent to us for people who have limited spaces in their room. This model is capable of displaying a 720p movie and has an amazing contrast of 2,000,000,000:1. You will be able to mount this model easily on the wall or just let it stand on top of your table.
