AT&T’s Cell Phone Tracker

February 4th, 2011 by GuestPoster

Tracking down your family can be done today quite easily. You will only have to choose the right cell phone tracker application. There are a lot of tracking apps which you can use but a really useful one is the one provided by AT&T. This is a great family tracking application and it also has quite a few helpful options. One of these options is the AT&T Family Map Schedule Checks. With these checks you will always be informed about the location of your children and even your spouse on a time which you will set. It is a really important option if you do not have time keep an eye on the computer at all times in order to see the location of your family.

The AT&T Family Map Schedule Checks are undeniably really helpful but I guess that you want to know how they work. Well, first of all you should select a day and an hour. It can be even a day span like only the weekend or the week days. Then you should select an hour for those days. After doing this you should select which person you want to tie to this hour and date. If you want to make sure that your kids are leaving school safely then you should set the time at which they normally finish their courses. Then tie this time to all the days from Monday to Friday and the tracking application will announce you were are the ones you want to track down at that time in that day. Now you will know all the time where your kids are.
