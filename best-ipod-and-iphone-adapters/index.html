Guide to the Best iPod and iPhone Adapters

August 12th, 2010 by GuestPoster

Own or thinking of getting an Apple iPod or iPhone? This guide will give you a brief insight into the variety of iPod and iPhone adapters you can find to make the most of your device!

Apple’s iPod rage is currently a hugely recognized brand and market leader. They’ve used this to their advantage by improving their product rage and features with each new year and version of the iPod – ranging from the Classic, to the Nano to their new iPod Touch and iPhones – which now feature far more advanced ‘applications’.

Being the market leader, Apple has created a huge business out of its accessories alone. Producing a large selection of accessories itself (such as an iPod car charger) and also allowing 3rd party’s to release them.

The use of many of the accessories comes from the very versatile ‘dock’ connection the iPod’s feature. This allows them to connect to a range of devices and interact with them. Some of which include:

- Connect to an external set of speakers

- Connect to an alarm clock/radio to wake up to your iPod music

- Charging docks

- Car adapters

Many of these devices are available from Apple themselves or from the 3rd party manufacturers. Generally, they will all work the same way with the 3rd party versions usually being cheaper.
